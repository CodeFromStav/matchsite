The Problem

Motivating newcomers to contribute to Open Source Software and retaining them in the project over time stubbornly remains a perennial weakness of OSS development.

The Solution

We will solve this by developing a robust mechanism for evaluate and reveal the gap between a newcomer’s skills and the skills required for a project on its work list.

Our Client

Our client is Dr Igor Steinmacher who completed his PhD in Computer Science at the University of São Paulo. Dr Steinmacher is an Assistant Professor/Researcher at Northern Arizona University who is currently working on helping support/motivate newcomers to contribute to Open Source Software.
